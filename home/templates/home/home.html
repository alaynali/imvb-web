About Us

The University of Illinois' Men's Volleyball Team is a registered student organization (RSO) and Club Sport at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. Our team competes in the National Club Volleyball Federation (NCVF) and the Midwest Intercollegiate Volleyball Association (MIVA). The club is entirely student-run by executive board members and team captains and consists of 3 teams.
