Teams


IMVB is divided into three teams: the 1s, 2s, and 3s. Club leaders determine teams at the beginning of the season based on skill level.
The 1s team competes in NCVF Division I A while the 2s and 3s compete in NCVF Division III against other B and C teams. All three teams maintain the goal of competing at a high level.
